Parental Consent Process

For users under 18, we require:

  • Parent/guardian identity verification with government-issued ID
  • Facial recognition matching between parent ID and selfie
  • Explicit consent for child's account creation and data processing
  • Understanding of child safety measures and monitoring

Information Collected from Children Under 18

We may collect and store the following types of personal information from minors only with verified parental consent:

  • Username and display name
  • Birth date (for age verification only)
  • Preferred language for translation
  • Parent's or guardian's contact information
  • Chat messages (stored with encryption and enhanced monitoring)

Parental Access Rights

Parents who have given consent can at any time:

  • Review, correct, or delete their child's personal information
  • Discontinue further collection or use of their child's personal information
  • Receive weekly activity reports about their child's platform usage
  • Immediately terminate their child's account

Types of Data Collected

Personal Data

While using Our Service, We may ask You to provide Us with certain personally identifiable information that can be used to contact or identify You. This may include:

  • Username and display name
  • Email address
  • Birth date (for age verification and COPPA compliance)
  • Preferred language settings
  • Chat messages and translated content
  • Age verification documents (processed by AI and immediately deleted)

Translation Data

As a translation platform, we process:

  • Original messages in your preferred language
  • Translated messages using DeepL API
  • Language preferences and translation history
  • Translation quality feedback and corrections

Age Verification Data

For safety and legal compliance:

  • Government-issued ID images (processed by OCR, immediately deleted)
  • Facial recognition data for identity verification (not stored)
  • Age confirmation results (verified/not verified status only)
  • Parental consent verification for minors

Usage Data

Usage Data is collected automatically and may include:

  • Device IP address and browser information
  • Time and date of platform access
  • Pages visited and features used
  • Translation usage patterns and frequency
  • Chat room participation (for safety monitoring)

Use of Your Personal Data

The Company may use Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain our Service: Including real-time translation and multilingual chat functionality
  • To manage Your Account: Registration, authentication, and account security
  • To ensure child safety: Age verification, parental consent, and content monitoring
  • To provide translation services: Processing messages through DeepL API for accurate translations
  • To contact You: Service updates, security notifications, and parental reports for minor accounts
  • For safety and moderation: Content filtering, abuse prevention, and compliance with child protection laws
  • To improve our Service: Analytics on translation accuracy and user experience (anonymized for minors)

Sharing of Your Personal Data

We may share Your personal information in the following situations:

  • With Service Providers: DeepL for translation services, cloud hosting providers (under strict data protection agreements)
  • For child safety: With parents/guardians for minor account activity reports
  • For legal compliance: When required by law, court orders, or to protect user safety
  • With Your consent: Any other sharing requires your explicit permission
